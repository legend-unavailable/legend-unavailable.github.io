Drum & Bass(DnB)

HOME

Ah, Drum & Bass(DnB), probably the most recognizable EDM sub-genre. Its main feature is its unique drum beat which uses breakbeats, which are looped, fast beat samples(about 165-185 BPM). Besides the beat, DnB uses synths, heavy bass with sub-bass lines, and of course, synths. Old DnB tends to be kind of slow and repetitive compared to the new DnB which tends to be more aggressive with its bass and more EDM like(build-ups and drops). To be honest, I don't really like DnB(new or old). Old DnB, as mentioned, is too slow and repetative for my ADHD brain and new DnB has good basslines but I don't think it goes well with its unique breakbeat style. Where DnB really stands out for me is one of its sub-genres: Dancefloor DnB. Dancefloor DnB is a more lighter, melodic, and danceable version of modern DnB. Dancefloor DnB is one of those feel-good music genres and it's honestly one of my favorties music genres of all time. Other sub-genres that I like include Drumstep, which I already mentioned before, and Metal & Bass which uses distorted electric guitars, the DnB style breakbeat, and occasionally harsh vocals. Some of my favorite artists include Feint, Maduk, REAPER, Zardonic, etc.
