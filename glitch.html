Glitch Hop

HOME

I can almost guarantee you haven't heard of this sub-genre. Glitch Hop doesn't have the same mainstream popularity as the other sub-genres but it does have a small niche among the gaming community. Glitch Hop is kind of a weird genre as its primary characteristic is its use of broken/glitchy sounds which makes it sound like it came from a video game. Its tempo is almost always at 110 BPM but the style of beats can vary. Glitch Hop is a versatile genre and as such, it can be mixed with other genres. Some artists mix it with Dubstep which gives it that deep kick while others give it a hip-hop type beat puting the hop in Glitch Hop. I'm not really knowledgeable on Glitch Hop sub-genres but I do know that Lo-Fi Hip Hop is derived from Glitch Hop as it has some glitch elements like that vintage vinyl sound. Some personal favorite Glitch Hop artists inlcude TheFatRat and Phaera.
